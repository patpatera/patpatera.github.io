A method for computation of a complete hash from a multimedia recording of a person's face is provided. The method includes detection and localization of a person's face in an input image sequence with computation of an averaged facial representation of the person's face. The method further includes detection of a person's voice in an input multimedia file with computation of an averaged voice representation of the person's voice. The method also includes transforming the detected person's face into a partial fingerprint. The method also includes transforming the detected person's voice into a partial fingerprint. The method further includes comparison of two partial face fingerprints and of two partial voice fingerprints by computing a similarity measure.
